    Jeevan

    Uniting Hearts and Healing Hands for Life
    About Jeevan
    The Jeevan project aims to simplify the process of finding organ and blood donors in today's busy world. Named after the Hindi word for "life". Jeevan is a platform designed to connect individuals in need with willing donors swiftly and securely. By leveraging user-friendly interfaces, Jeevan ensures efficient coordination. This innovative approach aims to overcome traditional barriers in donor matching, potentially saving numerous lives and enhancing emergency medical response capabilities.
    Features of Jeevan
    Jeevan simplifies healthcare accessibility with three core features. The Blood Match feature enhances blood donation logistics by allowing users to find nearby donation centers easily, optimizing scheduling for emergency healthcare. OrganMatch streamlines organ donation requests, providing a seamless platform for patients to request transplantations and match with compatible donors. DonorConnect portal empowers donors to pledge organs and blood through intuitive registration, maximizing their impact on lifesaving efforts.
    Blood Match
    Revolutionizing Blood Donation Logistics
    The Blood Match feature of Jeevan optimizes the logistics of blood donation, enabling users to easily locate nearby hospitals and donation centers. It facilitates direct contact for seamless donation scheduling, addressing critical supply chain challenges in emergency healthcare.
    Organ Match
    Streamlined Organ Donation Requests
    Organ Match on Jeevan provides registered patients with a straightforward platform to request organ transplantations. Through secure channels, patients can submit their needs, and help the hospital match them with suitable donors based on compatibility and urgency, ensuring timely and efficient access to life-saving treatments.
    Donor Connect
    Empowering Organ and Blood Donors
    Donor Connect feature on Jeevan empowers donors to pledge both organs and blood. Registered donors can easily sign up, specify their donation preferences. This feature ensures that donors play a crucial role in potentially life-saving procedures, contributing to improved healthcare outcomes and emergency response capabilities.

    Technologies We Used To Build Jeevan
    Jeevan is built using a robust tech stack designed for efficiency and scalability. The backend is powered by Python and Django, ensuring a reliable and scalable framework for handling complex data and user interactions. MariaDB serves as the database management system, providing a reliable and high-performance database solution. The frontend utilizes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create an intuitive and responsive user interface. Together, these technologies enable Jeevan to deliver a seamless experience to everyone utilizing Jeevan
