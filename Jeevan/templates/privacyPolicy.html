    Privacy Policy

    At Jeevan, we understand the importance of protecting sensitive personal and health-related information. Our commitment to privacy is paramount, especially given the nature of our service involving organ and blood donations. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our website and services.

    Information Collection

    We may collect various types of information from you, including but not limited to:

    • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
    • Health-related information (blood type, medical history relevant to donation)
    • Location information (for finding nearby donation centers)
    • User-generated content (donation pledges, organ/blood donation preferences)

    This information is collected through:

    1. Direct input during registration or profile creation
    2. Interactions with our website and mobile application
    3. Cookies and other tracking technologies

    Purpose of Data Collection

    We collect and use your information primarily for the following purposes:

    1. Facilitating organ and blood donation processes
    2. Matching donors with recipients
    3. Providing location-based services for finding donation centers
    4. Improving our platform and services
    5. Communicating with users regarding donations and system updates

    Data Protection

    Jeevan employs robust security measures to protect your sensitive information:

    • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
    • Secure authentication protocols
    • Regular security audits and penetration testing
    • Compliance with relevant healthcare data protection regulations

    Data Sharing

    Your information may be shared under the following circumstances:

    1. With medical institutions and healthcare providers for the purpose of facilitating donations
    2. When required by law or to comply with legal processes
    We will never sell or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

    User Rights

    As a user of Jeevan, you have the following rights regarding your data:

    1. Right to access and review your personal information
    2. Right to opt-out of certain communications
    Please contact our privacy officer at
    jeevanproject@proton.me
    to exercise these rights.

    Data Retention

    We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above. After termination of your account, we may retain some information for legal and regulatory compliance purposes.

    Changes to Privacy Policy

    Jeevan reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any significant changes will be communicated through our website and/or email notifications.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please don't hesitate to contact us at
    jeevanproject@proton.me
