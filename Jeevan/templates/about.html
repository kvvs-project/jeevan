    About Jeevan

    Uniting Hearts and Healing Hands for Life

    At Jeevan, we believe that every individual deserves access to life-saving medical care. Our mission is to bridge the gap between those in need of organ and blood donations and willing donors, fostering a community of compassion and generosity.

    Our Story

    Jeevan was born out of a passion to make a difference in people's lives. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to leverage technology to streamline the organ and blood donation process. Today, we're proud to be part of a global movement towards better healthcare accessibility.

    Our Mission

    To revolutionize the way organ and blood donations are facilitated, making life-saving resources available to those who need them most.

    Our Values

    1. Compassion: We put empathy at the heart of everything we do.
    2. Innovation: We continuously seek ways to improve our services and impact.
    3. Integrity: We operate with transparency and honesty in all our dealings.
    4. Community: We foster connections that save lives.

    Our Impact

    Since our inception, Jeevan has:

    • Facilitated hundreds of successful organ donations
    • Connected hundreds of blood donors with patients
    • Reduced average wait times for organ transplants by 400%
    • Increased blood donation rates in Pathanamthitta by 70%
